Digital Innovation Challenge for women 2017 is a competition, organized by “Women In Digital” together with some selected companies. The goal of this event is to encourage women for the Ideas development. It’s challenged not only about the development of apps, websites and online services but also provide real value of these development to the communities.

This competition program is aimed to make all women, think as an entrepreneur. It is worthy for those women who are interested in the field of digital innovation. The main purpose of our program is to motivate all talented and creative women (competitors) be act like startups or specialized as well as come up with revolutionary ideas or product for the digital challenges of the partner companies.

Competition duration will be for one (01) month. Contestants must be available in this time period and the judges/mentors will sit with contestant to guide them. According to the guidance contestant must complete the idea/project till the deadline.

Individuals (women) and groups around the country (Bangladesh) are eligible to participate as long as their ideas, applications, websites or services are designed to meet the contest criteria. The language of the prototype idea and all accompanying materials including the entry need to be in English or Bangla. The projects are not necessary to be finished. It can be simply a prototype too. For any type of queries about “Digital Innovation Challenge for Women 2017”, able to find at challengeinfo@widbd.com. Subscription of challengeinfo@widbd.com can keep ones update about the upcoming session.
