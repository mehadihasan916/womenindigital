Women in Bangladesh special in rural areas are disadvantaged in their opportunities. We want to make them independent. There is a huge need for smart, skilled workforce in the field of Bangladesh Digital Transformation. And women talents can make significant contributions to this. History shows that this does not happen automatically. That is why we have set up project 'Women in Digital'.
Women in Digital has already achieved a lot. But there is still a long way to go. And we cannot and will not do it alone. Read below what Women in Digital has already achieved and what can be expected soon.

In Bangladesh we have several educational center’s to educate and train girls and women in information technology. We provide training programs varying from data entry to database management. During Covid, we experienced a major setback in the development of our training capacity. Unfortunately we had to close some centers. But our vision did not change and we are desperate to rebuild these and to move forward again.
 

The Female Tech Database in our initiative to register all BD Women with an IT background. Educated by Women in Digital itself of not, you are welcome in our database.

Companies that want to employ (more) digital talents to diversify and strengthen their IT department can search talents here.

By Source, Women in Digital is servicing a number of companies outside BD, mainly in web development and graphic design. We have clients in the UK, India, Austria, Australia and Germany.

We provide female digital power by supplying foreign clients with dedicated and established Agile scrumteams of 4 to 8 specialists.
 

LuminaDev is a female-led agency, proudly operating out of Bangladesh. It was founded in 2013 by Achia Nila, an internationally recognized technology leader who also serves as an IT Expert for the International Telecommunication Union and the Country Director for Women who Code. Nila founded Lumina Digital alongside Women in Digital, a training center empowering women in Bangladesh with advanced technology skills.
Women in Digital, with the cooperation of customers, managed to acquire the intellectual property rights to some of the systems it made. With LuminaDev, these products are now being made generic and suitable for the BD market and later international markets. We are well advanced with a system for on-line invoicing .

Later applications can be expected in the area of Water Billing, Doctor’s Appointments en Pharmacy Sales and Stock and more.
 
Educating and adding 100.000 extra women to Bangladesh’ Digital Transformation is ambitious. Women in Digital cannot do this alone. Read on to see how you can help.

Women in Digital is looking for partners that help us rebuild and expand our remote training capacity or can join us in accomplishing our mission.
 

LuminaDev and Source are social responsible entities that work on a commercial basis and provide our talents with reasonable salaries that allow them to build independence. Yet we manage to be very competitive, especially in the international market. We are always looking for more organizations that support our vision and want to experience what our added value can be.
